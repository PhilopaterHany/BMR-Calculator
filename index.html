What is BMR ?

Basal Metabolic Rate is the number of calories your body uses to maintain vital functions, such as breathing, heart rate and brain function.

Why is BMR important ?

BMR indicates the number of calories a person burns at rest. This info could be helpful for a person who is trying to manage their weight.

How to calculate my BMR ?

BMR can be calculated by many equations, but the most accurate one is Harris-Benedict - Revised By Mifflin and St.Joer . You can calculate your BMR with the calculate at the bottom of the page.

What do I need to calculate my BMR ?

BMR depends on person's age, height, weight and gender; therfore everyone has a different BMR.

What Is BMI ?

Body Mass Index is a value derived from the mass (weight) and height of a person.

Why is BMI important ?

Your BMI is a measurement that is a ratio of your weight and height. It's a good way to guage whether your weight is in healthy proportion to your height. In fact, knowing your BMI can help you determine any health risks.

How Can I Calculate My BMI ?

You can calculate your BMI with the calculator at the bottom of the page.

What do I need to calculate my BMI ?

BMI depends on person's height and weight; therfore everyone has a different BMI.
